Father Michael's Roastery

I may be the only Orthodox Priest roasting coffee and possibly the first Orthodox Priest you’ve ever heard of. For the past ten years my family and I have been blessed to serve the nuns of Saint John’s Greek Orthodox Monastery in Goldendale, Washington. I want to pass that blessing on to you: from my hand to your cup. Who doesn’t need a little blessing now and then?

Guatemala Antigua Los Volcanes:

Full City Roast (Medium)

"This is a remarkable coffee whether your day is just beginning or ending. Goes well with that big glazed donut or those little plastic chocolate ones you choke down while driving to work."

Mexico Terruno Nayarita Natural Organic:

City Roast (Medium -)

Blueberries, blueberries, blueberries. This is a good simple cup of coffee that exudes a fruity character with strong blueberry notes. Medium-bodied and well balanced acidity with a smooth finish.

Need a break from the exotic knock-me-over coffee and just want to mellow out with a little fruity, well here it is. Not bad with your favorite cake donut but not to be paired with those little plastic chocolate ones. Powdered type ok.

Indonesia:

Sulawesi Toraja:

Vienna Roast (Dark)

This coffee is deep and intense with an earthy taste and low acidity. Blending the melange preserves the nuttiness in the vienna roast with the the deeper vienna+ roast

For those who aren’t scared of the dark. This rich cup stirs my visions of coming downstairs and seeing dad in his half tied robe coffee cup full, paper in hand and cigarette barely hanging on his lips with an ash ledge about two inches long. Truly a man’s cup. Not what mommy would drink.

Ethiopia Natural Limu ~Nigusie Lemma:

Full City Roast (Medium)

Limu is the coffee varietal, Nigusie Lemma is the farmer in the Mitto Gunim region, and natural means it is dry-processed. Ethiopia Limu is a fruity and sweet coffee at any roast, and at full city it offers a nicely balanced cup, with peaches and plums, the dry-process gives it what you can usually expect, a wine-like complexity, and finally some nice nuttimess, almond oil that gives it a round depth, which goes nicely with its overall brown sugar and subtle spiciness. It is very likeable, and satisfying for those who like to look for interest.

Custom Blends:

Goldendale Blend:

Full City Roast (Medium +)

This is my favorite all around full bodied blend. It’s so good that I named it after our beloved town of Goldendale. In this blend we bring together the South and Central Americas with the addition of a sturdy Indonesian flare, and a hint of a uniquely aged India Monsooned Malabar, giving it a mellow musty flavor.

Eastern Queen:

City Roast (Medium -)

This is the apple of our eye, a finely blended coffee from three distinct Far East regions. "Our Eastern Queen" adorned in blue has an aroma of licorice that is a fully rounded cup of coffee filled out like fresh honey and fixed with points of lime, mango, maple, and white pepper. Enjoy her company.

Father’s Moka Java:

City/ Full City Roast (Medium)

This is our rendition of the age old Moka Java blend; the Moka part gives its acidity and fruitiness, and the Java part gives its nuttiness and depth.
